Before you get started with ToggleFinance, we need to learn more about your business as a part of our KYC process.

What is KYC?

Know Your Customer guidelines in financial services require that professionals make an effort to verify the identity, suitability, and risks involved with maintaining a business relationship.

What is the KYC process?

The process for digital KYC is as follows:

1. Collection of documents: The first step in the KYC procedure is to collect all the required documents about the said parties/ business to get started with the actual verification
2. Uploading the documents: After the information is collected, the applicant is required to validate the information they put in the form with relevant documents. These documents serve as evidence proving that the prior information entered by the user is authentic and not fake.
3. Verification: Once the documents have been uploaded, the template for the documents is both identified and examined against various checks. This ensures that the document has not been tampered with in any way.

What documents should be submitted to fulfill KYC process?

The KYC process at ToggleFinance requires a user to upload two sets of documents under Business Documents and Financial Fitness Documents. You may need to submit all or some of the following documents according to your category:

Business Documents
1. Certificate of Incorporation of the Company.
2. Memorandum and Articles of Association of the Company
3. Tax Registration Certificate of the Company
4. Business Permit/License
5. List of Directors
6. Passport or ID Card copies of each of the Directors
7. Tax Registration Certificate of each of the Directors
8. Premises Utility Bill or Property Lease Agreement

Financial Fitness Documents
1. Bank Statements for the Company for the last 6 months
2. Company audited Financial Statements for last 3 Financial Years or certified mobile money Financial Statements for
the last 12 months
